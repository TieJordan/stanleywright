Your Voice Matters
Our politicians are out of touch and have been leaving this district behind for their own self-interest. Like you, I am tired of being forgotten and as your representative I will always fight for what matters to you.

Public Safety
We need to restore public trust in law enforcement and ensure safe neighborhoods through increased police presence and addressing the mental health crisis. The state must work with local and federal governments and non-profits to try to combat this issue together.

Jobs & Economy
With inflation out of control, our neighbors are concerned about financial stability, placing food on the table, and keeping the heat on. We need to expand opportunities for families to obtain higher paying jobs without being saddled with huge debt. We can do this by prioritizing increased access to career and technical schools, and keeping taxes down to encourage new business investment and stability.

Supporting Students, Parents, & Teachers
It's time to raise our education standards and ensure that every Alaskan child is given a true chance to succeed. By improving fundementals like reading and science, we can inspire creativity and a love for learning. Addressing this issue starts with parent involvement and more stability for our educators.
