Supporting Students, Parents, & Teachers
We need to raise our education standards and ensure every Alaskan child is given an adequate education. Starting with the fundamentals like reading and STEM to inspire creativity and a love for learning. This requires parent involvement and providing stability for our educators.

Protecting a Sustainable PFD
I fully support enshrining a 50/50 split in the Alaska Constitution for the PFD, and a constitutional spending cap which forces the government to live within our revenue means (no more unsustainable deficits).

Keeping Statewide Taxes Out of Alaska
We have plenty of resources in Alaska. We should not introduce a sales or income tax. We have a spending problem that needs to be addressed. If Alaskans are taxed further, government will spend even more money on a bloated budget!

Addressing the Mental Health Crisis
We are experiencing a mental health crisis in this state. Many people suffer from a lack of availability of resources and support. The State should be working with all parties (local government, federal government, and non-governmental organizations) to try and combat this issue together.

Creating Jobs for Everyone
We need to develop our resources, build businesses and infrastructure, which will lead to jobs and greater revenue.
